Introduction

In 1576 the Dutch United Provinces at last declared their independence from Spain. Over time, their power and influence grew and they acquired great commercial as well as sea power. In 1815 the Kingdom of the Netherlands was formed after a brief French occupation.

During the first World War, the Netherlands stayed largely neutral, but during the second World War were unfortunately occupied by the Gernmans. Now, the Netherlands are one of the founding members of N.A.T.O. as well as the E.U. .

Communication

Broadcast Media

In the Netherlands, most households are connect to satelliete or cable TV systems and are as such able to watch a wide variety of shows on channels both domestic and foriegn. There are also almost six hundred radio channels.

Telecommunication Systems

At the moment, the Dutch telecom market is going through an upgrade as they transition to better equipt platforms. Operators are adapting to the times by making the necessary changes to their spectrum and physical assets for LTE and 5G.

Internet Users

In the Netherlands, there is a total of 16.4 million internet users as of 2021. As of 202o that is 91.33% of the populaton.
