About Me

Dominique Kalunga is songwriter, composer, and novelist born in the D.C. area. She is currently a sophmore at the University of Miami, where she is double majoring in Music Composition and Computer Science with a minor in Music Business and Songwriting. Dominique is versed in the piano, which she has played for 16 years, the saxophone, which she has played for 10 years, and the flute, which she has played for 6 years. She songwrites mainly in the Musical Theatre style, and is currently working on an original musical called Legend. She is also working on a fantasy trilogy, the first enstallment of which she plans to traditionally publish by the end of her sophmore year.
